HARD RE-RANKING QUERIES (lexical overlap everywhere; semantics must decide)
H1: "Our site needs MW and MW and frequency support"  (intentionally messy)
    Good answers discuss power capability, energy duration, and fast frequency
    products; distractors talk about data-center MW or audio frequency.

H2: "We want 95% round-trip efficiency for our battery project."
    Prefer nuanced chunks defining boundaries; penalize universal single-number
    claims unless qualified.

H3: "Tell me about zinc batteries for the bulk power system."
    Most relevant might discuss technology alternatives generally; penalize the
    hearing-aid zinc-air distractor unless the re-ranker is fooled by keywords.
